Shoulder Abduction Instructions

Sit upright with your arms relaxed by your side and your palm facing toward you. Slowly raise your arm out to the side while keeping your elbow straight.

Continue lifting until your arm reaches approximately shoulder height or slightly above. The target shoulder abduction angle should be around 100°–105°.

Keep your back straight, avoid leaning your body, and perform the movement in a slow, controlled manner. Then slowly lower your arm back to the starting position.
